Project overview

I was asked to illustrate the concept of a questionable China’s commodities sector activities. The cover story’s focus was a company nicknamed ‘the Qingdao Whale’. After a lot of brainstorming and trying out different compositions, I decided that the cover illustration should mainly consist of what is under water. I chose whale as a focal point of the illustration.
MY ROLE: art director, illustrator, designer.

Process

I sketched a number of different versions for the same concept. After researching into and trying different styles of illustration, I decided that for the Chinese themed cover paper cut style illustration will be best suited. Once the winning drawing was selected I scanned and finalised the hand-drawn sketch in Adobe Illustrator.
