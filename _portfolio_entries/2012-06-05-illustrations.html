Project overview

I was tasked to produce a cover illustration and a five page layout for the cover story of the Global Trade Review (GTR) magazine. Cover story layout

I enjoyed designing the layout for the cover story because it gave me a chance to extend the story further. I went for a two column layout on a seven column grid (leaving one column empty). This gave me more space on the page meaning I could come up with the design that felt continuous from one page to another. Road was a very helpful element to achieve that.
