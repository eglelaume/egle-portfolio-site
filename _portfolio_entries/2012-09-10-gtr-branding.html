Project overview

Global Trade Review (GTR) is event organiser for the global trade, commodity, export and supply chain finance industries. Whilst working there I designed various marketing collateral that was used prior and, also, at their events. My day-to-day tasks included creating brochures, leaflets, certificates, thombstones, large format artwork, presentations and motion graphics. Please see some pictures below showing the products I designed that where used at different GTR events.
With up to thirty events per year there was a lot of marketing materials to be produced. I would sometimes need to meet extremely tight deadlines in order to send artwork with the latest updates to print in time.
In order to ensure marketing collateral is consistent from event to event, I would work from InDesign templates to produce any artwork.
Although working from templates could become quite a dull task, I think there is always space for improvement. My team and I review images after each event and try to see if there is anything that could be changed to look better.
