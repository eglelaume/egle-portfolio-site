The Economist

Web, UX

Eleven Tigers is electronic music producer based in London. I was asked to design Eleven Tigers branded USB flash drive that had to look like a tiger head. Additionally, I had to deal with printers from China to manufacture the product. The limited edition flash drive was used to hold the second Eleven Tigers album called 111.

Prototyping

After discussing client's vision for the product I went straight into Google SketchUp and started prototyping. You can see the snippets from the 3D product drawings as they were supplied to printers below.

The result

I went for an extremely stylized tiger head design incorporating prominent 111 logo on the front. Around two hundred flash drives were dispatched worldwide to Eleven Tigers fans who crowd-funded the album.
