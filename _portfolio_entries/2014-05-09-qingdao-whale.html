Project overview

Global Trade Review (GTR) is a publisher and event organiser for the global trade, commodity and supply chain finance industries. I was tasked to illustrate the GTR magazine cover story that investigated questionable China’s commodities sector activities and a company nicknamed ‘the Qingdao Whale’. Whale has a great shape so it was a delight to explore and draw it.

My role: art director, illustrator, graphic designer.

Approach

Ideally, the drawing had to include some ships or harbour to reflect on trading aspect of the story and it also had to illustrate the concept of searching or hidden.

I started off with sketching a number of different cover versions and after the winning drawing was selected I scanned and finalised the hand-drawn sketch in Illustrator. You can see the final sketch versus finalised .pdf below.
