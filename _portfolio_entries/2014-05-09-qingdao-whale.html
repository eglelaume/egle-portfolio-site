Project overview

I was asked to illustrate the concept of questionable China’s commodities sector activities. The cover story’s focus was a company nicknamed ‘the Qingdao Whale’ so it was a no brainer to make whale a focal point of the cover. Whale has a great shape so it was a delight to explore and draw it.

My role: art director, illustrator, graphic designer.

Approach

Ideally, the drawing had to have some ships or harbour to reflect on trading aspect of the story and it also had to illustrate the concept of searching or hidden.

I started off with sketching a number of different cover versions and after the winning drawing was selected I scanned and finalised the hand-drawn sketch in Illustrator. You can see the final sketch versus finalised .pdf below.
