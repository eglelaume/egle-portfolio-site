I design to make people's lives easier and happier.

Experience

I am a Los Angeles based UX Design Director with 6+ years of in-house and agency-side experience, working with clients such as The Economist, McKinsey & Company, City of Santa Monica and Faraday Future.


I enjoy solving complex problems and advocate for data driven and strategic design process that puts the user at the center while considering business needs.


I currently work at Edenspiekermann agency, where I own and guide my team’s design processes, methodologies and activities from concept through to finished product. I enjoy coaching my team through challenges as much as being a hands-on member of the team at all stages of the project.


While at Edenspiekermann, I have created digital products and experiences for brands in automotive, technology, government, publishing and finance. My most recent project includes leading a team of six product designers in creating UI for 10+ unique interactive screens within Faraday Future’s electric vehicle.


I hold MA in Publishing from the University of the Arts London. I have given design critique of students’ work at California State University, Long Beach and ran a Design Sprint at McKinsey & Company.
